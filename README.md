# SPITBALL

**Where words land. Forever.**

Spitball is a social network with one rule: you cannot delete anything.

- **No backspace.** Each keystroke is permanent.
- **No drafts.** Your words are live the moment you type them.
- **No delete.** Nothing you post can be removed.
- **No media.** Text only. Always.
- **No edit.** What you typed is what you meant.

---

Posts are called **spits**. You publish by clicking **Spit it** — but the content was locked in letter by letter. Likes are called **Marks** and are permanent. Your username is set once and cannot be changed.

The aesthetic is high-contrast black and white. No colors. No rounded corners. No friendly UI. Just your words, forever.

---

Built with plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No framework. No backend. Everything lives in your browser's localStorage.
